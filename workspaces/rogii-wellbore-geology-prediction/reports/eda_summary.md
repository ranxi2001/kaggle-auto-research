## Target and IDs

`TVT` is the continuous target. Evaluation rows are precisely those where `TVT_input` is missing.
All observed-prefix `TVT_input` values match `TVT`. Submission IDs are stable original row positions,
not positions after sorting: `{well_id}_{row_index}`. Predictions are therefore generated by ID and
left-joined to the runtime sample submission order.

## Leakage Controls

- Excluded horizontal columns: `TVT`, `ANCC`, `ASTNU`, `ASTNL`, `EGFDU`, `EGFDL`, `BUDA`.
- Excluded auxiliary inputs: typewell `Geology`, PNGs, and any public-test/train-ID target lookup.
- Allowed horizontal inputs: `MD`, `X`, `Y`, `Z`, `GR`, and observed `TVT_input`.
- Allowed reference inputs: typewell `TVT` and `GR`, which are present for hidden inference.
- Split boundary: entire wells are assigned to a single GroupKFold fold.
- Score boundary: only natural missing-suffix rows are included in OOF RMSE.

## Modeling Implications

`TVT_input` is never observed on both sides of a gap, so ordinary interpolation reduces to a
last-known-value forecast. Simple linear extrapolation from MD or Z was unstable and substantially
worse in early checks. The useful signals are the prefix anchor, 3D well path, horizontal GR, and
alignment to typewell GR. The chosen recipe combines an explicit sequence alignment with a strongly
regularized residual tree model.

The visible test set overlaps three training well IDs and is not representative of the hidden rerun.
Visible-test RMSE is not reported as candidate evidence.
